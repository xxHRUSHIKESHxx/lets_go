Happy Birthday To The Love Of My Life ❤️

As the clock ticks past midnight, I find myself staring at the screen, my fingers hovering over the keyboard. "Happy Birthday," I type, each letter a silent prayer, a whisper of longing that stretches across the miles between us. It hurts, knowing that I can't be there to see the smile on your face, to feel the warmth of your hand in mine as you make a wish. But in these words, in this message, I'm pouring all of my hopes, all of my dreams of a future where we'll celebrate together. I may not be there today, but I promise you, with every beat of my heart, that I'll make it up to you. One day soon, we'll blow out those candles together, and our love will shine brighter than any distance could ever dim. Happy Birthday, my love.
